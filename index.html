About Us

Grassroots Advocacy for Tinubu 2027
(Enugu Chapter)

Grassroots Advocacy for Tinubu 2027, Enugu Chapter is a people driven movement dedicated to mobilizing communities across Enugu State through civic education, grassroots engagement, and inclusive political participation.

We work at ward, local government, and state levels, connecting everyday citizens to national leadership through dialogue, awareness, and responsible advocacy in support of President Bola Ahmed Tinubu's leadership vision toward 2027.

Our Mission

To mobilize, educate, and engage grassroots communities across Enugu State, promoting informed participation and collective action through peaceful, people centered advocacy.

Our Vision

An engaged and politically aware Enugu State where grassroots voices actively shape national progress and democratic leadership.

Our Core Values

Grassroots Inclusion

Every voice matters

Civic Education

Awareness before action

Unity and Dialogue

Progress through collective understanding

Democratic Responsibility

Peaceful, lawful participation

Community Impact

Leadership that connects to local realities
